Description

Kimberley Times is newspaper company that we assure it will expand worldwide after a few years. They specialize in Entertainment, Weather, Technology, Africa, and Sports. The newspaper is committed to giving people the news that will reflect creativity and growth.The Newspaper is based at Kimberley and dominating the field in Northern Cape

Purpose

The Purpose of Kimberley times is to bring the new around the world to Community of Kimberley online.This will help to fight the Global warning and land pollution in kimberley.The primary goal of our website is to increase the reading footprints online. The secondary goals which will support our primary goal is firstly by making our website as interactive as possible because we believe that by effectively interacting with our audience, our audience will have more trust in our newspaper in brining accurate and impartial news to them. The website interaction should be so good that the sales online are higher than the sales of the physical newspaper because e-commerce is a growing and convenient way of doing business. Lastly, our website should be able to gather more subscribers by at least 5000-10000 people every week. All this should be achievable in time frame of 6-11 months.
