The Mall of Africa Will Make Use of KaChing

Olebogeng,Itumeleng

2021 November 10

KaChing has been used by the Mall of Africa to allow customers to pay for parking.

KaChing parking customers can securely pay for their parking by using flexible payment options such as pre-pay, top-up, and credit card payment.

The system employs automatic number plate recognition cameras and smartphone app technology to allow people to pay without the use of a ticket or cash.

In 2016, the South African parking technology provider was established.

KaChing currently operates in Johannesburg at Melrose Arch, Morningside Shopping Centre, Thrupps, Campus Square, and the Pavilion Shopping Centre, with 10 more locations set to open soon across the city, according to Durba.
